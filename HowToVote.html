How To Vote

Prepare for the Election

  1. Election day is Tuesday, March 3, 2020.
  2. Make sure you REGISTER TO VOTE!
  3. Check your registration status here.
  4. If you are not registered to vote, you can do so here.
  5. Now that you are ready to vote, you can decide whether you want to vote in person or by mail.

How to Vote

There are two ways to vote: In person or by mail.

  • If you're voting in person you need to find your polling place here.
  • If you're voting by mail you need fill out a vote-by-mail request here. Or by calling the County Registrar's office.

Go and Vote

  • If you're voting in person, on Tuesday, March 3, between 7:00AM - 8:00PM vote at your location.
  • If you're voting by mail, fill out your ballot and return by mail or to the County Registrar's office by March 3rd.
